Editor's note · November

"I do not know what walks are for. I keep doing them anyway."

This issue collects three months of looking. There is a long essay at the heart of it that I have re-written more times than I would like to admit; the rest is the slow accumulation that makes the essay possible — photographs from a wet afternoon in Yaba, a dawn walk along the bridge, fragments from a notebook I cannot keep tidy.

I have begun to think of this site less as a publication and more as a room. The reader is welcome to wander; nothing has to be read in order. Most of what is here is small. The point, I think, is the smallness.

— P.P., Lagos · 21 Nov 2025
